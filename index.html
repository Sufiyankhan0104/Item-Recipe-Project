BURGER KING

Ingredients

  • ½ cup mayonnaise (preferably Hellmann's or Best Foods)
  • ⅓ cup ketchup (preferably Heinz)
  • 2 tablespoons sweet relish
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped cornichons
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped red onion

Recipe

  1. Lay the bacon in a medium cast-iron skillet and cook over medium heat, turning once, until golden and crispy, about 5 minutes per side.
  2. From the top and 3 sides of the head of lettuce, cut 4 thick round slices, each 4 to 5 inches across, to use as buns and set aside.
  3. Shape the ground beef into 2 patties, each about 4 inches across and 3/4 inch thick, and sprinkle both sides with salt and pepper.
  4. Heat the skillet over high heat and once hot, add the patties and cook for 3 minutes per side for medium-rare.

CHLLI POTATOES

Recipe

  1. Wash and clean the potatoes, cut them into thin long pieces and put aside.
  2. Boil some water in a pan and add salt to it.
  3. Drop in the cut potato pieces and boil them until they are soft but not mushy.
  4. Strain the potato pieces and keep them aside. Let them cool down completely.
  5. Dust the boiled potato pieces with some corn flour and keep them aside.
  6. Pour oil for deep frying in a kadai and heat it up.
  7. Drop in the coated potatoes gently and then fry them for approximately 3-4 minutes until they turn golden brown in colour.
